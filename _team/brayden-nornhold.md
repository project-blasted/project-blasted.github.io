**Biography**  
Brayden is a Junior Meteorology major at Millersville University. Originally from Halifax, Pennsylvania, he has enjoyed the weather ever since he was little. This fascination culminated with a job shadow at ABC27 News in 2019, cementing the hobby as a promising career path. Upon graduation, Brayden wants to pursue a path in operational/broadcast meteorology with an emphasis on winter weather. Outside of the major, Brayden enjoys spending time with friends/family, playing video games, and observing the weather.
