**Biography**  
Brendan White is a fourth-year meteorology major and hydrology minor at Millersville University. Hailing from Brambleton, Virginia, Brendan has always had a curiosity for the natural world, always seeking answers to how the world works and following his passions. His primary interests in meteorology include severe weather, winter weather, tropical weather, and flash flooding. After college, Brendan's goal is to work for the National Weather Service as a forecaster to continue following his love for forecasting and to help keep people safe. Outside of class, Brendan plays club lacrosse at MU and spends time at the gym or listening to music.
