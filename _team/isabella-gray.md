**Biography**  
Isabella Gray is a sophomore meteorology major with environmental hazards/emergency management and mathematics minors at Millersville University. Growing up right outside Reading, Pennsylvania, Isabella became fascinated with The Weather Channel and any weather-related book she could get her hands on. As she grew older, Isabella became curious about studying the processes behind forecasting and preparedness when issuing weather information for the public. After graduation, she hopes to work in aviation or government forecasting to improve the accuracy of weather alerts and protect lives and property. Outside of meteorology, Isabella plays cymbals as an assistant drumline captain in the Millersville University Marauder Marching Band and teaches cymbals as an Indoor Percussion Technician at Wilson High School in West Lawn, PA. She also enjoys reading all genres of books, drawing in color books, listening to music, and spending time with her family.
