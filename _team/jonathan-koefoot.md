**Biography**  
Jonathan Koefoot is a meteorology major and an emergency management major at Millersville University. Jonathan Koefoot’s passion for meteorology started at a young age, watching storms and encountering countless thunderstorms at summer camps. Through the years, he has been through countless storms and even witnessing the landfall of Hurricane Isaias on the North Carolinian coast. Outside of his studies, Jonathan Koefoot is currently employed through Millersville University’s IT Help Desk and enjoys working on computers and other electronic gadgets. Outside of university, Jonathan Koefoot enjoys playing video games with his friends, chasing/filming trains of all types, and currently is developing a hobby of nature/weather photography.
